About Me

I’m Dave Stevens. Thank you for visiting my personal web site. I design and build web applications that help companies, both large and small, achieve their goals.

Over the years I’ve had the privilege of working with several creative and successful teams. I’ve been involved in hundreds of projects, creating quality solutions for our customers.

However, this doesn’t mean I like to sit back and manage projects while everyone else has all the fun. I have learned that the secret to keeping up to date on new technology is embodied in one simple statement, “never stop coding.” Why? Because the day I stop coding is the day I stop learning new things. And discovery and craftsmanship are what excites me the most about the field of information technology.

Educational Background

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Iowa State University back in 1990. I have continued my technical education through various conferences and user groups.

But to be honest, I learn the most through doing: prototyping and being involved in cutting edge projects.
